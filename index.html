About Sanabil

Jamia Islamia Sanabil, located in Shaheen Bagh, New Delhi, is a leading Islamic seminary founded in 1980 by Maulana Abdul Hameed Rahmani. Run by the Abul Kalam Azad Islamic Awakening Centre, it offers education from Maulwi to Fadil, combining Islamic studies with modern subjects in a well-equipped campus.

Calssrooms

Jamia Islamia Sanabil's classrooms blend traditional and modern education, offering Islamic studies alongside English, Math, and Science. Guided by experienced teachers, students develop spiritually and intellectually in a disciplined environment designed to promote learning, critical thinking, and character building.

Library

Sanabil's library houses a vast collection of Islamic and academic books. Students explore Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, Arabic, and modern subjects in a peaceful setting that encourages reading, research, and reflection. It's a vital space for nurturing knowledge and scholarly excellence.

Sports

Sanabil promotes fitness and discipline through sports. Taekwondo is professionally taught by Ajmal Hussain (Black Belt Dan-3, National Poomsae, Judge Refresh). Football, volleyball, and badminton are enjoyed under Master Nusratullah's supervision, helping students build teamwork, confidence, and physical strength.

Computer Classes

Fadhil students at Sanabil receive computer training through DOAP and ADOAP programs. Courses include MS Office, InPage, Photoshop, CorelDRAW, and internet use. These skills empower students to use technology effectively for academics, Dawah, and future career opportunities.
